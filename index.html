Good to know

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

How long does a full home interior take?

Most full-home projects are designed, manufactured and installed within about 45 days from design sign-off. Timelines vary with scope, material choices and site readiness.

Is the first consultation really free?

Yes. Your first consultation and initial 3D design concept are completely free, with no obligation to proceed.

Do you handle both design and execution?

We do. Zesta is end-to-end — one accountable team takes you from first sketch through manufacturing, installation and final handover.

What warranty do you offer?

Our modular products carry up to a 10-year warranty, with quality-checked materials and a dedicated project team throughout.
