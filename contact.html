Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you might want to know before getting started.

How long does a full home interior take?

Most full-home projects are designed, manufactured and installed within about 45 days from design sign-off. Timelines can vary with scope, material choices and site readiness.

Is the consultation really free?

Yes. Your first consultation and initial 3D design concept are completely free, with no obligation to proceed.

How is pricing calculated?

Pricing depends on the scope of work, materials, finishes and the size of your space. After your consultation you receive a transparent, itemised quote with no hidden costs.

Do you offer a warranty?

Yes. Depending on your package, we offer a 5 to 10 year warranty on our modular products and workmanship.

Which cities do you serve?

We currently design and execute projects in and around Bengaluru, Pune and Hyderabad, and are expanding to new cities.
